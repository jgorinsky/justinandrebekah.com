DIRECTIONS

Forest Park can be confusing to navigate, even to long term St. Louis residents. And Justin. We hope the turn-by-turn directions below will see you safely to your destination!

From The Cheshire

  • Take a right out of the parking lot onto Clayton Road, heading east.

  • Almost immediately, take a slight right onto Clayton Avenue. The AMOCO station will be on your left.

  • At the stoplight, take a slight left onto Oakland Avenue.

  • Continue on Oakland Ave for 0.6 miles, then take a left on Tamm Avenue.

  • Take a right onto Wells Drive and the Zoo's South Parking lot will be on your right.

From Moonrise Hotel

  • From the Moonrise parking lot, turn right onto Delmar Boulevard, heading west.

  • At the stop light, turn left onto Skinker Boulevard.

  • Continue on Skinker for 1.5 miles, and turn left onto Wells Drive, just before the stoplight.

  • Continue on Wells Drive for 1 mile and the Zoo's South Parking lot will be on your right.

From Drury Inn

  • Exit the Drury's parking lot and turn left at the stoplight, onto Hampton Avenue heading north.

  • Continue on Hampton for 1.1 miles, passing over I-64/40 and entering Forest Park.

  • When you get to the traffic circle, take the third exit onto Wells Drive.

  • Continue on Wells Drive for 0.7 miles and the Zoo's South Parking lot will be on your left.
